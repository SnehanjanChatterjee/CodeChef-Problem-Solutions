All submissions for this problem are available.Given two sentences, if there is at least one common word in the two sentences print 
"Yes" else print "No". 

Input:
T
Sentence 1A
Sentence 1B
Sentence 2A
Sentence 2B
....
...
Sentence TA
Sentence TB

Output:
Result for Sentence 1A and 1B
Result for Sentence 2A and 2B
...
Result for Sentence TA and TB

Constraints:
1<=T<=10
1<=Number of words in a sentence<=50
All words will be in lowercase

Example:
Input:
2
going to two
two of you
who are you
I am gone

Output:
Yes
No
